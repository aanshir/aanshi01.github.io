Inter School Quiz Competition

We are proud to share that Chickar International School achieved 1st Position in the Inter School Quiz Competition held at BBL Public School, Bareilly in which boys and girls of the city’s top schools participated.
Our team comprised of Mili Chandola (Class XII) and Gauri Agarwal (Class X).
We congratulate the girls on an outstanding performance! #GirlPower

Counselling Session 2017

As always, the most important session was where the parents were counselled to step out of the stereotyped, comfort zones and advised on the various options available to their children.
Based on their abilities and aptitude, the students of Class X were guided on the choice of subjects while the students of Class XII were guided on the various options available to them for their chosen subjects.

CIS at Model United Nations

We are proud to share that the girls of Chickar International School participated as delegates at La Martiniere Girls’ Model United Nations Conference held in Lucknow between 15-17 June, 2018.
Rubbing shoulders with teams from the leading schools of the country, it was a great experience for our students as they learnt the working of the United Nations.
